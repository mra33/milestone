My Work

Improv Comedy & Acting

After graduating with a degree in Business Administration I went to Second City in Chicago and took classes in improvisation, acting and voice over. In LA I studied improvisation at UCB.

Stand Up Comedy

I produced, hosted and performed with Sleeping with Comedy a weekly open mic with a booked show after. We also created a storytelling show and a variety comedy show.

Figure Skating & Dance

I grew up as a competitive figure skater and dancer. In high school I was President of the Figure Skating Club and captain of Dance Team. I take dance classes in LA and still figure skate.

About Me

I’m known for being a friend to people and for improv, stand up, figure skating, acting, writing and dance.

I truly love comedy. There is no better feeling than making someone laugh. I’m very excited for this coming year, I have a couple projects in the works and I’m so excited to share them.
